Portuguese Tarlet Recipe

Ingredients
  1. Sugar Syrup
  2. 1 3-4-in cinnamon stick
  3. 1/2 cup sugar
  4. 1/2 cup water
  5. Filling
  6. 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  7. 1/8 tsp kosher salt
  8. 1 1/2 cups whole milk, divided
  9. 6 large egg yolks
  10. 1 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Puff pastry sheet
Prep
  1. Thaw puff pastry if in the freezer for 40mins, fridge OKAY
  2. Preheat the oven to 400F, tack in the middle of the oven
  3. Peel zest from half a lemon with a vegetable peeler, put it aside
For the Syrup
  1. Bring cinnamon, sugar, and water to boil in a small saucepan
  2. Boil for 1-2mins or 225F if you have a thermometer
  3. Remove from heat and stir in the reserved lemon peel
  4. Sit 30 mins
For the Egg Custard Filling
  1. Whisk flour, salt, and 1/2 cup milk in a bowl until no lumps remain
  2. Heat remaining cup of milk in a saucepan over medium-high heat til it begins to boil, about 5 mins
  3. Remove the hot milk from heat and whisk into flour mixture
  4. Return the mixture to saucepan over medium heat and cook, stir occasionally, until thick, creamy, and smooth, about 5 mins
  5. Strain sugar syrup through a fine-mash sieve into hot milk mixture and whisk to combine
  6. Whisk in egg yolks and vanilla
Assembly
  1. Cut chilled puff pastry dough to whatever the shape of your baking container is like
  2. Lightly sprayed the baking containers if you buy the puff pastry from store
  3. Using your thumbs and fingers, press edges of dough against sides of cup, turning pan as you go, until dough comes halfway up sides of cup and is about 1/16" thick (or as thin as you can get it). Repeat twice more with remaining dough slices. (DO NOT PRESS TOO MUCH if you want them to be flaky)
  4. Filling each pastry shell 3/4th way up the sides
Bake!
  1. Bake in oven for 15 minutes (for the size of 10oz custard cups, increase time to 20minutes if size doubles)
